Hello, my name is Giang

I’ve always been driven by curiosity — curiosity about how things work, why people make certain choices, and how data can reveal patterns that aren’t immediately visible. This natural interest grew into a passion for understanding the intersection of data, technology, and business.

My journey began with exploring how numbers tell stories. Over time, I realized that data isn’t just about analysis — it’s about translating technical findings into insights that guide meaningful decisions. Whether it’s optimizing a process, predicting an outcome, or understanding customer behavior, I find excitement in uncovering the “why” behind the data.

Compiled a Python-based ETL pipeline with Pandas, SQL and Spark to extract, clean, and store drug information, and utilized Hive for efficient querying and up-to-date aggregation of large datasets

AdoptGraph: Social Tech Uptake Forecasting

Forecasted technology adoption among Facebook users by analyzing 100K+ social graph nodes and edges, incorporating features such as friend count, interaction frequency, and neighbors’ behavior

Implemented a 3-layer Graph Convolutional Network with PyTorch Geometric3-layer Graph Convolutional Network with PyTorch Geometric, reaching a baseline accuracy of ~80%

Extended analysis with global graph signals, including connection density and average node clustering to enhance prediction
